Methodology

What “validated” means here.

A factor only earns the validated label when it clears a strict research process that reduces data-mining risk and improves interpretability.

01

Out-of-sample testing

Performance is measured only on held-out data and sequential walk-forward splits.

02

Significance control

Adjusted tests account for the number of candidate specifications explored during research.

03

Decay analysis

Information content is tracked across horizons to detect fragile or artefactual signals.

04

Robustness checks

Signals are stress-tested across periods, regimes, and alternative construction choices.
