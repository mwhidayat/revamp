Methodology

How Every Factor is Built

We apply a structured research workflow that eliminates overfitting and ensures statistical credibility before any factor is released.

01

Hypothesis Generation

Each factor begins with an economic rationale — a testable hypothesis grounded in market structure, investor behaviour, or fundamental accounting relationships. We do not begin with data mining.

02

Screening & Construction

The factor is constructed on a defined universe with explicit rules for signal computation, neutralisation, and winsorisation. All construction choices are documented prior to testing.

03

Out-of-Sample Validation

The final model is tested exclusively on held-out data never seen during development. We use sequential, walk-forward splits to prevent data leakage.

04

Decay Analysis

We measure how quickly each factor's information content decays across holding horizons. Factors with rapid unexplained decay are rejected or flagged for review.

05

Robustness Checks

Factors are stress-tested across sub-periods, market regimes, and alternative signal specifications. A factor must survive perturbation to qualify for release.
